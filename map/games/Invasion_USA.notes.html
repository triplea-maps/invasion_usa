Gameplay
  • When game startes there will be an initial deployment phase before turn 1 begins.
  • The Americans first places all its 60 units (24 Infantry, 9 Mechanized, 12 Armor, 9 Helicopters and 6 Bombers) when the game starts. It has to place 2 units on all of the 30 US cities.
  • Starting with Western each invader then places 20 predefined units, on its attack zones: 8 Infantry, 3 Mechanized, 4 Armor, 3 Helicopters and 2 Bombers. Each invasion zone has a unit placement limit of 5.
  • After all the invaders place their 20 units turn 1 will start. The order of play for each turn is: Western, Southern and Eastern, with the Americans playing last.
  • The turn sequence is: Develop Reinforcements/Place Reinforcements/Combat Move/Battle/Non Combat Move.

Americans Objectives
  • Americans player objective: control at least 13 cities until turn 12.
  • The Americans receive 1 Lase Tower at the beginning of each turn to place into any Americans controled city.
  • The Americans also have the chance to recive additional military unit by use of Technology Rolls.
  • Americans cannot move into the starting invasion zones.
Invader(s) Objectives
  • Invader player(s) objective: conquer and hold at least 18 cities at the end of the Americans turn. After which the invader will be at war with each other.
  • The invaders are not allied and can attack and take territories from one another, after a declaration of war. However, they cannot move into the others' invasion zones.
  • A Single invade is the winner by controlling 13 cities on its own.
